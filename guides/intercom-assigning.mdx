<Note>
  Parahelp is assigned conversations like any teammate in Intercom. Once assigned, it works the conversation until it's resolved, and only transfers when its configurations say it should or it can't resolve the issue.

  You can assign conversations automatically using [Intercom workflows](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/7836459-workflows-explained).
</Note>

## 1. Review your existing workflows

1. In Intercom, go to **Fin AI Agent → Workflows**.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-24.png)
</Frame>

2. Check how new conversations are routed today:

- Live chat workflows sit under **When customer opens a new conversation in the Messenger**.
- Email workflows sit under **When customer sends their first message**.

{/* SCREENSHOT: Intercom workflows overview */}

# 2. Set up workflows to assign new conversations to Parahelp

You can set up Parahelp workflows either by creating completely new workflows (option A) or adapting your existing workflows (option B).

## Option A: Create new workflows

### Live chat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the workflow">
    Click **New workflow → Create from scratch**.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-76.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the trigger">
    Choose **When customer opens a new conversation in the Messenger**.

    <Frame>
      <Frame>
        ![Image](/images/image-28.png)
      </Frame>
    </Frame>

    Select the Messenger channels Parahelp should handle.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-29.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to Parahelp">
    Add an **Assign** step.

    <Frame>
      ![Image (15)](/images/image-\(15\).png)
    </Frame>

    Assign the conversation to the Parahelp teammate.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-31.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Click **Save**. When ready, click **Set live**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the workflow">
    Click **New workflow → Create from scratch**.

    <Frame>
      ![Image (16)](/images/image-\(16\).png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the trigger">
    Choose **When customer sends their first message**.

    <Frame>
      <Frame>
        ![Image (17)](/images/image-\(17\).png)
      </Frame>
    </Frame>

    **S**elect **Email** as the only channel.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-34.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to Parahelp">
    Add an **Assign** step.

    <Frame>
      ![Image (18)](/images/image-\(18\).png)
    </Frame>

    Assign the conversation to the Parahelp teammate.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-26.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Click **Save**. When ready, click **Set live**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
