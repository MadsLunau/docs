title: "Assigning Conversations in Intercom"

<Note>
  Parahelp is assigned conversations like any teammate in Intercom.

  Once assigned, Parahelp works the conversation until it is resolved and only escalates when its configurations outline that it should or cannot resolve the issue.

  You can automatically assign conversations to Parahelp using [Intercom workflows](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/7836459-workflows-explained).
</Note>

## 1. Overview of your existing workflows

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workflows">
    In Intercom, go to **Fin AI Agent → Workflows**.

    <Frame>
      ![Image (13)](/images/image-\(13\).png)
    </Frame>

    {/* SCREENSHOT */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check how new conversations are routed today">
    - Live chat workflows sit under **When customer opens a new conversation in the Messenger**.
    - Email workflows sit under **When customer sends their first message**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 2. Set up workflows to assign new conversations to Parahelp

You can set up Parahelp workflows either by creating completely new workflows (option A) or adapting your existing workflows (option B).

### Option A: Create new workflows

#### Live chat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the workflow">
    Click **New workflow → Create from scratch**.

    <Frame>
      ![Image (14)](/images/image-\(14\)-1.png)
    </Frame>

    {/* SCREENSHOT */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the trigger">
    Choose **When customer opens a new conversation in the Messenger** as the trigger.

    <Frame>
      ![Image (15)](/images/image-\(15\)-2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the channels">
    Select the Messenger channels Parahelp should handle.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 06 07 At 12 06 09](/images/Screenshot_2026-06-07_at_12.06.09.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an Assign step">
    Add an **Assign** step.

    <Frame>
      ![Image (16)](/images/image-\(16\)-1.png)
    </Frame>

    {/* SCREENSHOT */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to the Parahelp teammate">
    Assign the conversation to the Parahelp teammate.

    <Frame>
      ![17](/images/17.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and set live">
    Click Save. When ready, click **Set live**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the workflow">
    Click **New workflow → Create from scratch**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the trigger">
    Choose **When customer sends their first message** as the trigger.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the channel">
    Select **Email** as the only channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an Assign step">
    Add an **Assign** step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to the Parahelp teammate">
    Assign the conversation to the Parahelp teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and set live">
    Click Save. When ready, click **Set live**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Option B: Adapting your existing workflows

#### Live chat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Duplicate the existing live-chat workflow">
    {/* SCREENSHOT */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rename the duplicate">
    Rename the duplicate workflow to **Parahelp: \[Workflow name\]**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change the assignment step">
    Change the assignment step so it assigns to the Parahelp teammate instead of the existing team or teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch over">
    When ready, pause the old workflow and set the new Parahelp workflow live.
  </Step>
