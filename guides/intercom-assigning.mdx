---
title: "Assigning Conversations in Intercom"
description: "Route conversations to Parahelp with Intercom workflows, like any teammate."
---

Parahelp is assigned conversations like any teammate in Intercom. Once assigned, it works the conversation until it's resolved, and only transfers when its configurations say it should or it can't resolve the issue.

You can assign conversations automatically using [Intercom workflows](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/7836459-workflows-explained).

## Review your existing workflows

In Intercom, go to **Fin AI Agent → Workflows** and check how new conversations are routed today:

- Live chat workflows sit under **When customer opens a new conversation in the Messenger**.
- Email workflows sit under **When customer sends their first message**.

## Option A: Create new workflows

### Live chat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the workflow">
    Click **New workflow → Create from scratch**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the trigger">
    Choose **When customer opens a new conversation in the Messenger**, and select the Messenger channels Parahelp should handle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to Parahelp">
    Add an **Assign** step and assign the conversation to the Parahelp teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Click Save. When ready, click **Set live**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the workflow">
    Click **New workflow → Create from scratch**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the trigger">
    Choose **When customer sends their first message**, and select **Email** as the only channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to Parahelp">
    Add an **Assign** step and assign the conversation to the Parahelp teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Click Save. When ready, click **Set live**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Option B: Adapt your existing workflows

Works the same for live chat and email:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Duplicate the existing workflow">
    Duplicate your current live-chat or email workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rename it">
    Rename the duplicate to **Parahelp: \[Workflow name\]**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reassign">
    Change the assignment step so it assigns to the Parahelp teammate instead of the existing team or teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch over">
    When ready, pause the old workflow and set the new Parahelp workflow live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
