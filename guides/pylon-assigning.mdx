---
title: "Assigning Issues in Pylon"
description: "Route issues to Parahelp with Pylon triggers, like any teammate."
---

<Note>
  Parahelp is assigned issues like any teammate in Pylon.

  Once assigned, Parahelp handles the issue until it is resolved and only escalates when its configuration outlines that it should or cannot resolve the issue.

  You can automatically assign issues to Parahelp using [Pylon triggers](https://docs.usepylon.com/pylon-docs/platform/triggers).
</Note>

## 1. Overview of your existing triggers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Triggers">
    In Pylon, go to **Settings → Triggers**.

    <Frame>
      ![Pylon Triggersettings](/images/pylon-triggersettings.png)
    </Frame>

    {/* SCREENSHOT */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check how new issues are routed today">
    You can usually see this in the trigger that triggers on "New issue created".
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 2. Set up triggers to assign new issues to Parahelp

You can either create a completely new trigger (option A) or adapt your existing trigger (option B) to assign new issues to Parahelp.

### Option A: Create a new trigger

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new trigger">
    Create a new trigger called "Parahelp: Assignment rules".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick when">
    Pick when **"New issue created"**.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 06 30 At 16 41 40](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-30-at-16.41.40-1.png)
    </Frame>

    {/* SCREENSHOT */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick then">
    Pick then **"Set issue assignee"** to **"Parahelp"**.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 06 30 At 16 44 17](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-30-at-16.44.17.png)
    </Frame>

    {/* SCREENSHOT */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the settings">
    Ensure **none** of the settings are enabled.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 06 30 At 16 44 37](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-30-at-16.44.37.png)
    </Frame>

    {/* SCREENSHOT */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the order">
    Make the "**Parahelp: Assignment rules**" trigger the first in your routing group.
  </Step>
</Steps>
