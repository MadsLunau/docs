The Manager is your side of Parahelp. Here you work with the **Manager Agent**: an AI agent you prompt in plain English to build, test, and improve your entire support setup. Nothing it proposes goes live without your approval.

## What it can do

- **[Tasks](/platform/chats):** ask anything in plain English: how a ticket was handled, how a policy works, what changed last week.
- **[Configuration](/customer-agent/configurations):** change how your Customer Agent behaves: update policies, memory, and knowledge, or build new tools.
- **[Testing](/customer-agent/testing):** test every change against real tickets before it goes live.
- **[Reporting](/customer-agent/reporting):** performance, bug patterns, knowledge gaps, or any slice of your ticket data.
- **[Automations](/platform/continuous-tasks):** put any of this on a schedule: daily reports, post-release checks, alerts when something ships.
- **[Releases](/platform/actions):** view all approved actions, versioned, and revertable.
- **[Connections](/internal-agent/tools):** act in your tools: update Linear issues, edit Notion pages, post to Slack, open GitHub PRs.
