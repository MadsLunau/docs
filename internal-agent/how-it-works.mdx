The Manager is your side of Parahelp. Here you work with the **Manager Agent**: an AI agent you prompt in plain English to build, test, and improve your entire support setup. Nothing it proposes goes live without your approval.

## What it can do

- **Change how your Customer Agent behaves:** update policies, memory, and knowledge, or build new tools, tested against real tickets before going live.
- **Answer questions:** how a ticket was handled, how a policy works, what changed last week.
- **Generate reports:** performance, bug patterns, knowledge gaps, or any slice of your ticket data.
- **Act in your tools:** update Linear issues, edit Notion pages, post to Slack, open GitHub PRs.
