---
title: "Ticketing System"
description: "Your Customer Agent works as a team member in Intercom, Zendesk, Front, Plain, or Pylon."
---

Your ticketing system is the main channel where tickets reach your Customer Agent. It replies, tags, transfers, and updates tickets directly in it, while the Manager Agent reads your tickets to answer questions, generate reports, and identify gaps. Parahelp supports one ticketing system per workspace.
