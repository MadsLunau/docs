<Accordion title="How tickets are counted" icon="sitemap">
  Every ticket falls into one branch:

  - **AI engaged**
    - AI resolved (general, spam, or merged)
    - AI assisted (handed to a human)
  - **AI not engaged**
    - Not routed to AI
    - AI ignored

  A resolved ticket is one the Customer Agent closed that stayed closed.
</Accordion>

## Ask the Manager Agent

The Manager Agent covers both the numbers and the why.

**Quantitative reports.** _"Show me a graph of resolved tickets over the past month"_ gets you an interactive chart, sortable by category. It can analyze any slice of your ticket data.

**Qualitative reports.** Patterns, trends, and representative quotes across your tickets. Common ones: performance reports, bug-pattern reports, and improvement reports.

## Analytics Page

The Analytics page (Settings → Analytics) shows both agents at a glance, filterable by date range.

**Customer agent:** credit usage, assigned tickets, engaged tickets, resolved tickets, engagement rate, and resolution rate, measured against both engaged and assigned tickets.

**Manager agent**: credit usage, requests, tasks, and actions, filterable by team member.

## In your ticketing system

Your Customer Agent works as a team member in your ticketing system, so your existing reports track it automatically: SLAs, CSAT surveys, agent-level dashboards. We can also share report templates with the key metrics we recommend for every supported system.
