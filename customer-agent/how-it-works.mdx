The Customer Agent works as a teammate in your ticketing system. It replies to customers, takes action, and transfers to a human when needed, following your knowledge, tools, and policies.

Here it is handling a support ticket in Intercom:

<iframe src="https://www.loom.com/embed/9a513f6f8e374e7d8ce4d13942776340" title="Customer Agent demo" style={{ width: "100%", aspectRatio: "16 / 9", border: "0", borderRadius: "12px" }} allowFullScreen />

You shape everything it does, and the [Manager Agent](/manager/introduction) does the heavy lifting: prompt it to change a policy, build a tool, or test a scenario, and it stages the change for your approval.
