Connections let the Internal Agent and the Customer Agent perform actions in the tools you already use. Manage all your connections from the "Connections" page in Settings.

## Ticketing systems

### Capabilities

The Internal Agent reads your support tickets to answer questions, generate reports, and identify knowledge or product gaps. The Customer Agent replies, tags, transfers, and updates tickets directly in your ticketing system. Parahelp supports one ticketing system per workspace.
