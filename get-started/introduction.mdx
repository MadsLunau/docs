Parahelp works inside your ticketing system as two agents.

The [Customer Agent](/customer-agent/introduction) resolves support tickets end to end, using your knowledge, tools, and policies.

The [Manager Agent](/manager/introduction) expands its abilities. You prompt it in natural language, it updates configurations, builds tools, and tests them against real tickets. Nothing goes live without your approval.

As your product changes, the Manager Agent keeps the Customer Agent up to date, learning from every ticket it handles.
