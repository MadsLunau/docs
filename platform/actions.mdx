When you approve a configuration change in a Manager Agent task, such as a customer follow-up, or a new Linear issue, it lands on the "Actions" page as a pending action. Nothing goes live until you approve.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-19.png)
</Frame>

## States

- **In review:** waiting for you to review.
- **Done:** approved and published or executed.
- **Issue:** blocked by a typo, validation error, or merge conflict. Use the **Fix issues** button to hand the issue back to the Internal Agent.

## Who can approve

Admins approve and publish actions. Operators can draft and propose, but not approve. See [Settings](/platform/settings).

The Actions page also shows all approved actions by your team, so you can see what's been published and by whom.
